# Funeral Brand Master Plan

## Strategic Goal

Build Nekrolog Lodz as a dual-pillar premium funeral brand:

1. local funeral services, ceremonies and full burial organisation
2. domestic and international transport of the deceased, with strong specialisation in bringing the deceased to Poland

The website should work at the same time as:

- a premium brand presentation
- a trust-building service platform
- an international SEO framework
- an urgent conversion tool
- a scalable foundation for future operational tooling

## Brand Positioning

### Core identity

- family-run funeral company from Lodz
- over 30 years of experience
- calm, dignified and practical support
- no upfront family payment where applicable
- strong competence in urgent domestic and international transport

### Two main pillars

#### Pillar 1: funeral services

- burial organisation
- ceremonies
- cremation
- formal support
- local trust in Lodz and nearby cities

#### Pillar 2: transport and repatriation

- domestic transport of the deceased
- international transport of remains
- bringing the deceased to Poland
- support for families abroad
- support for Polish families dealing with a death abroad
